# Map 2: Recycling Fields - Quest Guide

Welcome to the **Recycling Fields**! This map focuses on the fundamentals of waste sorting, material processing, and circular economy principles.

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to complete every quest in this map.

---

## 1. Waste Crisis
**Quest Giver:** Wally Waste (Waste Manager)  
**Objective:** Sort at least 3 items correctly in the sorting facility.

### How to complete:
1. Walk around the map and look for the different resource piles (Plastic, Glass, Metal, Paper, and Compost).
2. Stand next to a pile and press **[E]** to gather waste materials into your inventory.
3. Locate the colored recycling bins grouped in the center/sorting area.
4. Approach the correct bin for your waste type and press **[E]** to sort it:
   * **Yellow Bin:** Plastic Waste
   * **Green Bin:** Glass Waste
   * **Grey Bin:** Metal Waste
   * **Blue Bin:** Paper Waste
   * **Brown Bin:** Organic Waste / Compost
5. Once you have correctly sorted 3 items, return to Wally Waste to complete the quest.

---

## 2. Know Your Materials
**Quest Giver:** Eco Emma (Eco Educator)  
**Objective:** Earn your Materials Expert Certificate.  
*Prerequisite: Complete "Waste Crisis"*

### How to complete:
1. Enter the **Eco Library** building (look for the "Library" sign).
2. Walk up to the bookshelves inside and press **[E]** to open the Lesson Selector.
3. Select one of the available materials lessons (e.g., Plastic & Polymers, Glass & Metals).
4. Read through the lesson pages and complete the quiz at the end by selecting the correct answers.
5. Upon scoring 100% on a quiz, you will receive the **Materials Expert Certificate** in your inventory.
6. Exit the library and speak to Eco Emma to hand over the certificate.

---

## 3. Circular Economy
**Quest Giver:** Materials Scientist  
**Objective:** Craft 1 Eco Brick.  
*Prerequisite: Complete "Know Your Materials"*

### How to complete:
1. Gather at least **3 Plastic Waste** and **3 Glass Waste** from the resource piles outside.
2. Enter the **Materials Lab** building.
3. Interact with the lab workbench.
4. First, process your raw materials:
   * Craft **Plastic Pellets** (requires 3x Plastic Waste).
   * Craft **Glass Cullet** (requires 3x Glass Waste).
5. Next, combine the Plastic Pellets and Glass Cullet at the workbench to craft an **Eco Brick**.
6. Exit the lab and speak to the Materials Scientist to complete the quest.

---

## 4. Composite Creation
**Quest Giver:** Materials Scientist  
**Objective:** Craft 1 Composite Wood Board and 1 Recycled Metal Ingot.  
*Prerequisite: Complete "Circular Economy"*

### How to complete:
1. Head back out to the fields and gather:
   * **Paper Waste** (from paper piles)
   * **Organic Waste** (from compost heaps)
   * **Metal Waste** (from metal piles)
2. Return to the **Materials Lab** and use the workbench.
3. Craft the **Composite Wood Board** by combining Paper Waste and Organic Waste.
4. Craft the **Recycled Metal Ingot** by processing your Metal Waste.
5. Speak to the Materials Scientist once again with both items in your inventory to finish the final main quest of the map!

---

*Completing all of these quests will unlock the portal to Map 3: EcoVille!*
