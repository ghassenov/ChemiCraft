# Atom Meadows — Foundational Chemistry

**Difficulty:** 1 | **Theme:** Bright meadow colors, floating pollen particles | **Portal unlocks after:** completing all 6 quests

---

## Learning Objectives

- Understand that all matter is made of atoms (protons, neutrons, electrons)
- Learn that atoms bond together to form molecules through covalent bonds
- Distinguish between single, double, and triple covalent bonds
- Read and interpret chemical formulas (H₂, O₂, N₂, H₂O, CO₂, CH₄, NH₃)
- Understand that atoms are conserved in reactions — rearranged, not created or destroyed
- Learn practical applications of common molecules: N₂ in air, H₂O as solvent, CH₄ as fuel, NH₃ as fertilizer

## Quest Chain

| # | Quest | Problem | Science Taught | Prerequisite |
|---|-------|---------|----------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Ventilation Crisis** | Village air is stale — craft N₂ | Nitrogen makes up 78% of Earth's atmosphere; triple covalent bond | — |
| 2 | **Water of Life** | Well is dry — craft H₂O | Water's molecular structure; two-step crafting (H₂ then H₂ + O) | Quest 1 |
| 3 | **Methane Harvest** | Generator needs fuel — craft CH₄ | Simplest hydrocarbon; tetrahedral geometry; natural gas | Quest 2 |
| 4 | **Ammonia Synthesis** | Need fertilizer — craft NH₃ | Haber-Bosch process; pyramidal molecular shape; nitrogen fixation | Quest 3 |
| 5 | **Carbon Capture** | Study CO₂ to understand greenhouse gases | CO₂ molecular structure; linear molecule; greenhouse gas properties | Quest 2 |

**Progression notes:**
- Quests 1–4 are strictly linear. Quest 5 branches from Quest 2.
- Difficulty ramps from diatomic (H₂, O₂, N₂) to triatomic (H₂O, CO₂) to polyatomic (CH₄ with 5 atoms, NH₃ with 4 atoms).
- Two-step crafting for H₂O (make H₂ first, then combine with O) teaches that some syntheses need intermediate products.

## Reagents & Molecules Crafted

| Molecule | Inputs | Bond Type | Real-World Use |
|----------|--------|-----------|----------------|
| H₂ | H + H | Single covalent | Rocket fuel, industrial feedstock |
| O₂ | O + O | Double covalent | Respiration, combustion |
| N₂ | N + N | Triple covalent | 78% of Earth's atmosphere |
| H₂O | H₂ + O | Two single covalent | Universal solvent, essential for life |
| CO₂ | C + O + O | Two double covalent | Photosynthesis, carbonated drinks |
| CH₄ | C + 4H | Four single covalent | Natural gas, heating fuel |
| NH₃ | N + 3H | Three single covalent (+ lone pair) | Fertilizer, cleaning products |
