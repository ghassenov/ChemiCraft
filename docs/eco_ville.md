# EcoVille — Climate Science & Sustainability

**Difficulty:** 3 | **Theme:** Green energy palette, wind particles | **Portal unlocks after:** completing all 5 quests

---

## Learning Objectives

- Understand the greenhouse effect and which gases contribute (CO₂, CH₄, N₂O)
- Learn the carbon cycle: photosynthesis, respiration, ocean absorption, fossil fuel disruption
- Understand carbon capture technology (chemical absorption, direct air capture)
- Learn about renewable energy: solar, biofuel, wind, hydropower
- Understand carbon neutrality: emissions = removal, and why it matters
- Recognize that climate solutions require interconnected, systemic approaches

## Quest Chain

| # | Quest | Problem | Science Taught | Prerequisite |
|---|-------|---------|----------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Carbon Neutral** | First carbon-neutral fuel — craft Biofuel | Biofuel is carbon-neutral: plants absorbed CO₂ while growing | — |

| 3 | **Clean Water** | Contaminated water — filter 3 samples into clean water | Activated carbon filtration | Quest 2 |
| 4 | **Renewable Shift** | Power the city — craft a Solar Panel | Photovoltaic effect; recycling → manufacturing supply chain | Quest 3 |

**Progression notes:**
- Strictly linear — tells one complete story from diagnosis to certification.
- The Green Certificate requires biofuel + solar panel + carbon credit — a system-of-systems synthesis.
